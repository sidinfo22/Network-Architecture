Cataloging hardware and software resources to uphold the healthcare office’s secure, efficient environment, meeting operational demands and strict compliance.

Assets & Inventory Overview

Having outlined our procurement and deployment strategies, the next step is to create a comprehensive view of all hardware, software, and licenses that form this healthcare network. Accurate tracking ensures we stay within budget, maintain compliance, and assign devices effectively across the organization.

Hardware & Device Tracking

Each piece of equipment is tagged with an RFID label or barcode, then scanned into our Asset Inventory System for real-time updates. This ensures full visibility into device locations, status, and usage.

Office Laptops & Desktops

Hostnames assigned based on department (e.g., HR-LT-001). Monitored for software versions, warranties, and user assignments.

Cisco Network Hardware

Includes routers, switches, and firewalls. Each device has a unique tag for quick identification. Configuration backups also associated with the same ID.

Mobile & BYOD Devices

Registered in the system to track which personal devices have access to corporate VPN or Wi-Fi. Ensures Zero-Trust policies remain enforced.

Software & Licensing

Alongside hardware, we maintain an inventory of software suites, OS licenses, and third-party applications crucial to daily operations.

  • Windows Server 2022 (20 licenses) – Domain Controller & local data services
  • Office 365 (30 user subscriptions) – Email & productivity tools
  • Cisco Security Suite (Firewall & IPS modules) – Subscription-based threat protection and updates
  • SQL Server 2019 (4 core licenses) – On-premises database & data warehousing

All software is tracked for version control and renewal dates, ensuring we remain compliant with licensing terms and retain full support coverage.

Inventory Management System

To streamline our asset documentation, we’ve selected Spiceworks Inventory for automatic device discovery, license audits, and real-time alerts. This cloud-integrated platform provides:

Live Reports

Instantly view asset distribution by department, device type, or user assignment.

Automated Alerts

Notifications when licenses approach expiry or new devices connect to the network.

Seamless Sync

Integrates with our helpdesk ticketing and Azure AD to keep user accounts and asset statuses up-to-date.
